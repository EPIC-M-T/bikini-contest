EPIC Bikini Contest: Official Terms & Conditions

By submitting your comp card, portfolio, and personal information for consideration in the EPIC Bikini Contest at Palm Tree Beach Club, you agree to the following Terms and Conditions.

1. Eligibility Requirements

  • Age: All applicants and contestants must be at least 21 years of age to participate. Proof of age and identity will be required if selected.
  • Valid ID: If chosen as a contestant, you must bring a valid government-issued ID to the event for age and identity verification. Failure to provide valid ID may result in removal from the contestant lineup.
  • Location & Availability: The contest takes place at Palm Tree Beach Club in Las Vegas, Nevada, on August 14th. Applicants must be available to travel to and attend the event.
  • Travel Costs: EPIC Models & Talent, official sponsors, event partners, and venue partners are not responsible for contestant flights, hotels, ground transportation, meals, parking, lodging, or any other travel-related expense unless a separate written agreement states otherwise.
  • Representation: Both agency-represented and self-represented models are welcome to apply. If you are agency-represented, it is your responsibility to ensure this submission does not violate your agency contract.

2. Selection Process

  • Contestant Count: Twenty-five (25) contestants will be selected to participate in the EPIC Bikini Contest.
  • Notification: Selected contestants will be notified by email. Selected contestants may also be announced on the official contest page, EPIC Models & Talent pages, and related social channels.
  • No Guarantee of Selection: Submitting an application does not guarantee selection, entry into the contest, or compensation. The casting team reserves the right to select contestants at their sole discretion.

3. Submission Materials & Accuracy

  • Minimum Requirements: A complete submission requires accurate physical statistics, current contact information, a valid ID upload, preferred headshot, and six (6) to ten (10) clear photographs.
  • Accuracy of Information: By applying, you certify that all submitted measurements, photos, age information, and contact details are current, accurate, and truly represent your appearance.

4. Media Rights & Usage

  • Submission Review: By uploading your photos and portfolio links, you grant EPIC Models & Talent the right to view, store, and share these materials internally for casting and selection purposes.
  • Selected Contestant Promotion: If selected, you authorize EPIC Models & Talent to post your name, image, public social handle, and contestant status on the main contest page and social channels.
  • Event Media: If selected to compete, you acknowledge that the EPIC Bikini Contest is a publicized event during Las Vegas Swim Week. You agree to be photographed, filmed, and recorded. Event organizers and sponsors retain the right to use this media for promotional, marketing, and commercial purposes in perpetuity.

5. The $10,000 Grand Prize & Sponsorships

  • Prize Structure: The advertised $10,000 Grand Prize represents the total value of cash, prizes, and potential sponsorship opportunities awarded during the contest.
  • Taxes: Any cash prizes or physical goods awarded are subject to federal, state, and local taxes. Winners are solely responsible for reporting and paying applicable taxes and may be required to fill out a W-9 before prize disbursement.

6. Code of Conduct

  • Professionalism: All selected models are expected to conduct themselves professionally. Harassment, intoxication, or disruptive behavior may result in immediate disqualification and removal from the premises.
  • Health & Safety: You agree to abide by all health, safety, and security protocols enforced by the venue and event organizers.

7. Liability Waiver

By submitting this form and/or participating in the contest, you release and hold harmless EPIC Models & Talent, venue partners, sponsors, affiliates, and employees from any claims, damages, or liabilities arising from your participation, including but not limited to personal injury, property damage, or travel-related incidents.

8. Contact Information

For any questions regarding your submission, contact the casting team at book@epicmodelsandtalent.com.
