EPIC Bikini Contest: Official Terms & Conditions

By submitting your comp card, portfolio, and personal information for consideration in the EPIC Bikini Contest at Tailgate Beach Club at Mandalay Bay, you agree to the following Terms and Conditions.

1. Eligibility Requirements

  • Age: All applicants and contestants must be at least 21 years of age to participate. Proof of age and identity will be required if selected.
  • Valid ID: If chosen as a contestant, you must bring a valid government-issued ID to the event for age and identity verification.
  • Location & Availability: The contest takes place at Tailgate Beach Club at Mandalay Bay in Las Vegas, Nevada, on August 14th, 2026. Applicants must be available to travel to and attend the event.
  • Travel Costs: EPIC Models & Talent, official sponsors, event partners, and venue partners are not responsible for contestant flights, hotels, ground transportation, meals, parking, lodging, or any other travel-related expense unless a separate written agreement states otherwise.
  • Representation: Both agency-represented and self-represented models are welcome to apply. If you are agency-represented, it is your responsibility to ensure this submission does not violate your agency contract.

2. Selection Process

  • Contestant Count: Twenty-five (25) contestants will be selected to participate in the EPIC Bikini Contest.
  • Notification: Selected contestants will be notified by email. Selected contestants may also be announced on the official contest page, EPIC Models & Talent pages, and related social channels.
  • No Guarantee of Selection: Submitting an application does not guarantee selection, entry into the contest, or compensation.

3. Submission Materials & Accuracy

  • A complete submission requires accurate physical statistics, current contact information, valid ID upload, preferred headshot, and six (6) to ten (10) clear photographs.
  • By applying, you certify that all submitted measurements, photos, age information, and contact details are current and accurate.

4. Media Rights & Usage

  • If selected, you authorize EPIC Models & Talent to post your name, image, public social handle, and contestant status on the main contest page and social channels.
  • If selected to compete, you agree to be photographed, filmed, and recorded at the publicized Las Vegas Swim Week event. Event organizers and sponsors retain the right to use this media for promotional, marketing, and commercial purposes in perpetuity.

  • All selected models are expected to conduct themselves professionally and follow venue, event, health, safety, and security requirements.
