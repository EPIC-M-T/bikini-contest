EPIC Bikini Contest: Privacy Policy

EPIC Models & Talent respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect the personal information you provide when submitting your application for the EPIC Bikini Contest during Las Vegas Swim Week.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit your application through our online form, we collect the following categories of information directly from you:

  • Contact & Identity Information: Your full name, age, email address, phone number, city/state of residence, and valid ID upload for age and identity verification.
  • Professional & Physical Information: Your physical statistics, Instagram handle, portfolio links, and agency or booking contact details.
  • Media: Any comp cards, headshots, identification image, or photographs you upload through our submission portal.
  • Additional Context: Any additional notes or information you choose to provide regarding your availability or recent work.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide strictly for evaluating your submission, selecting contestants, communicating with you, and organizing the EPIC Bikini Contest. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Review your look, experience, and suitability for the EPIC Bikini Contest.
  • Communicate with you or your agency regarding your submission status, event logistics, and potential selection.
  • Verify your identity and ensure you meet the minimum age requirement of 21+.
  • Notify selected contestants by email.
  • Publish selected contestant names, images, and/or public social handles on the official contest page, EPIC Models & Talent pages, and related social channels.
  • Maintain an internal casting file for the Las Vegas Swim Week contest team.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, give away, or otherwise transfer entry data or personal information for unrelated third-party marketing. We only share information with parties directly involved in casting, operating, promoting, or fulfilling the contest:

  • The Casting Team: Internal staff at EPIC Models & Talent who evaluate submissions.
  • Event Partners: Personnel who assist in final selections, venue logistics, event operations, security, or contestant check-in.
  • Official Contest Promotion: If you are selected, your name, likeness, submitted/public photos, public social handle, and contestant status may be posted on our main page and social channels.
  • Sponsors: If you are selected, limited professional information, such as your name, public photos, and Instagram handle, may be shared with official contest sponsors for promotional and prize-fulfillment purposes. We will not share your private phone number, email address, ID, or private contact information with sponsors without your consent except where required for prize fulfillment or legal compliance.

4. Data Security & Storage

We implement reasonable security measures to protect uploaded photos and personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Your media files and form data are routed to internal systems used for contest review and communication. No data transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and you submit information at your own risk.

5. Data Retention

We retain submission materials for the duration of the casting process and Las Vegas Swim Week contest operations. If you are selected, your data may be kept on file for event communication, contestant verification, prize administration, and event promotion. If you are not selected, we may securely retain your comp card and contact details for future casting opportunities unless you request deletion.

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Update Your Information: If your measurements, representation, or contact details change, you may contact us to update your file.
  • Withdraw & Request Deletion: You may withdraw your submission at any time. Upon request, we will delete your uploaded photographs and personal contact information from our active casting database, subject to legal, operational, or completed promotional obligations.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request deletion of your submission, please contact our casting team at book@epicmodelsandtalent.com.
