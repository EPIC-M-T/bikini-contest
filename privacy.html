EPIC Bikini Contest: Privacy Policy

EPIC Models & Talent and Palm Tree Beach Club ("we," "us," or "our") respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect the personal information you provide when submitting your application for the EPIC Bikini Contest during Las Vegas Swim Week.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit your application through our online form, we collect the following categories of information directly from you:

  • Contact & Identity Information: Your full name, age, email address, phone number, and city/state of residence.
  • Professional & Physical Information: Your physical statistics, including height, measurements, shoe/dress size, hair/eye color, Instagram handle, portfolio links, and agency or booking contact details.
  • Media: Any comp cards, headshots, identification image, or photographs you upload through our submission portal.
  • Additional Context: Any additional notes or information you choose to provide regarding your travel availability or recent work.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide strictly for the purposes of evaluating your submission and organizing the event. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Review your look, experience, and suitability for the EPIC Bikini Contest.
  • Communicate with you or your agency regarding your submission status, event logistics, and potential selection.
  • Verify your identity and ensure you meet the minimum age requirement of 18+.
  • Maintain an internal database of talent for the Las Vegas Swim Week casting team.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We only share your information with parties directly involved in the casting and execution of the event:

  • The Casting Team: Internal staff at EPIC Models & Talent who evaluate submissions.
  • Event Partners: Key personnel at Palm Tree Beach Club who assist in final selections or coordinate venue logistics and security.
  • Sponsors: If you are selected as a finalist, limited professional information, such as your name, photos, and Instagram handle, may be shared with official contest sponsors for promotional and prize-fulfillment purposes. We will not share your private contact information, such as phone or email, with sponsors without your consent.

4. Data Security & Storage

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your uploaded photos and personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Your media files and form data are routed to our secure internal systems. However, no data transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and you submit this information at your own risk.

5. Data Retention

We retain your submission materials for the duration of the casting process and the Las Vegas Swim Week event. If you are selected, your data will be kept on file for ongoing communication, payment processing, and event promotion. If you are not selected, we may securely retain your comp card and contact details for future casting opportunities unless you request their deletion.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Update Your Information: If your measurements, agency representation, or contact details change, you may contact us to update your file.
  • Withdraw & Request Deletion: You may withdraw your submission at any time. Upon request, we will delete your uploaded photographs and personal contact information from our active casting database.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, how we handle your physical statistics and photos, or if you wish to request the deletion of your submission, please contact our casting team at:
